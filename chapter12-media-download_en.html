Download Steps

  • Single Video Download:
    • Copy YouTube video URL
    • Paste into URL input field
    • Click "Media Download" button
  • Playlist Download:
    • Copy playlist URL

    • System automatically identifies and batch downloads

12.4 Common Issue Resolution

Figure 12-2: Common Issue Resolution Guide

Download Issues

  • Download Failed:
    • Check network connection
    • Confirm URL correctness
    • Retry download process
  • Audio Quality Issues:
    • Confirm original video quality
    • Check conversion settings
    • Ensure sufficient bandwidth

Playlist Issues

  • Some Videos Cannot Be Downloaded:
    • Check video availability
    • Confirm regional restrictions
    • Skip unavailable items
  • Naming Issues:
    • Avoid special characters
    • Check filename length
    • Manually rename if needed
